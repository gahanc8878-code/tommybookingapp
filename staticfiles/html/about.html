What Is A Tommy?




🢀 This Is A Tommy

A Tommy is a handsom, attractive, and charitable person, who used the electric power of his 300 magnamites to power a childrens hospital when their power went out and their backup generator failed. Due to his heroic actions, he was awarded the Nobel Peace Prize and has been a beloved figure in the community ever since. He is known for his kindness, generosity, and willingness to help others in need. Many people look up to him as a role model and aspire to be like him in their own lives. He has also done many other charitable acts, such as letting Ethan use one of his many Lmborghinis when his car broke down, or letting Callum sleep in one of his many mansion's when his house burned down. Did we also mention he saved Kim Kardashians pet goldfish from drowning?
